About Me

I have a strong passion for creating websites. I have a lot of experience in most aspects of software development, from creating scripts in BASH to crawl websites for important court docket data to creating back-end auditing using SQL and C# to monitor the changes of dynamic EMS and Fire electronic forms. Out of all of the software I've worked with, I've gotten the most pleasure out of designing something and seeing it come to life in visual form. It all started with creating games in C++ and the Allegro game library when I was 16. I bought C++ for Dummies on Amazon at the beginning of summer and had sleepless night after sleepless night, coding game engines. As I entered college, I learned a lot of algorithms and practices and honed my skills.
